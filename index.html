IxDF

"Why do i want to put my heart, mind and hard work into this particular job", that's a great question.


Well i'm not going to say things like pay or benefits, as this is kind of obvious, and quite frankly, somewhat crass as well. I would say that i'm very interested in working on a project that's directed towards learning. I always wanted to work on any website for online courses, training, exercises, etc. I once developed a small app for schools, and i had a blast trying to work out the many different relations between teachers, students, classes, subjects, grading, and everything else. It was a fun exercise.


Something else that i'm on the lookout for is a highly structured and well organized work environment, that upholds the law of respect, that is considerate, caring, with diversity and the team well being at the forefront.


I'm passionate about developing. Even if it wasn't my bread and butter, i would probably still develop and create software, be it apps or web applications, just for the kick of it. I'm proud of myself for having persevered and endured over the years, growing with every new client, project and employer, picking up knowledge whatever i went and with whatever technology i was asked to, always improving and refining all my knowledge. And i believe that with IxDF i'll have probably my best opportunity to grow.
